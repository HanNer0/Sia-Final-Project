Quick Summary

Epic: The Musical is a modern reimagining of Homer’s The Odyssey by Jorge Rivera-Herrans. Fusing hip-hop, pop, and musical theatre, it follows Odysseus and his crew on a thrilling, emotional journey home from the Trojan War. Told in sagas like The Troy Saga and The Cyclops Saga, the musical has captivated audiences across platforms with its cinematic storytelling and viral songs.
